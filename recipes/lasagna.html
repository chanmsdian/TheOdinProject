Lasagna

This classic lasagna is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family, and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Pepper
  • Ground Beef
  • Ricitta cheese
  • Garlic
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Onoins
  • Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook two lasagna noodles according to the package directions, drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the meat in a small skillet along with chopped onions, minced garlic, and the seasonings. Add in li the sauce and simmer.
  3. Layer the lasagna by lining a small baking pan or oven safe dish with the noodles as shown in the li pictures. Top with the meat mixture, sauce, and the cheeses.
  4. Fold the noodles over the meat mixture.
